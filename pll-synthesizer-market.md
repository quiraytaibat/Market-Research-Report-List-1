<p><strong>The PLL Synthesizer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Integer-N</li><li>Fractional-N</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PLL synthesizer market is divided into Integer-N and Fractional-N types. Integer-N synthesizers use integer multiples of the reference frequency, providing simplicity and speed, making them ideal for applications requiring low phase noise. Conversely, Fractional-N synthesizers allow for finer frequency resolution by using fractional multiples, enabling more precise frequency tuning, suitable for applications like wireless communications. While Integer-N offers reliability, Fractional-N provides flexibility, catering to diverse market needs in telecommunications and consumer electronics.</p></p>
