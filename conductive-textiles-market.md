<p><p>The conductive textiles market comprises three main types: woven, non-woven, and knitted textiles. Woven textiles are created by interlacing yarns, offering durability and strength, ideal for applications like smart clothing. Non-woven textiles are produced by bonding fibers together, providing flexibility and versatility, suitable for health and safety applications. Knitted textiles involve interlooping yarns, resulting in stretchable and breathable materials, which are popular for wearable electronics. Each type caters to diverse applications in industries such as fashion, healthcare, and automotive.</p></p>
