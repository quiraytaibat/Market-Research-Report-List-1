<p><ul><li>Radical UV-curable Resin</li><li>Cationic UV-curable Resin</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The UV curable resin market for optical discs includes two main types: radical UV-curable resin and cationic UV-curable resin. Radical UV-curable resins employ free radical polymerization to create durable coatings and bonding agents, ideal for applications requiring quick curing under UV light. In contrast, cationic UV-curable resins utilize cationic polymerization, providing enhanced chemical resistance and thermal stability, making them suitable for high-performance optical applications. Both types contribute to improved performance and longevity of optical disc products.</p></p>
