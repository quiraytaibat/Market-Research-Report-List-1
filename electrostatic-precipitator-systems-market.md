<p><p>Electrostatic precipitator systems are crucial for air pollution control by removing particulate matter from industrial emissions. The dry electrostatic precipitator operates by charging particles and collecting them on oppositely charged plates, making it suitable for high-temperature applications. In contrast, the wet electrostatic precipitator uses moisture to enhance particle collection, effectively capturing smaller particles and mists, making it ideal for industries producing wet or sticky emissions. Both types play vital roles in improving air quality and meeting regulatory standards.</p></p>
