<p><p>Chromatography pumps are essential in the separation and analysis of compounds, with their market primarily categorized into fluid and supercritical fluid types. Fluid chromatography pumps typically handle liquid solvents to facilitate the movement of samples through the chromatographic system. In contrast, supercritical fluid chromatography pumps utilize supercritical fluids, which possess properties of both liquids and gases, enhancing separation efficiency and sensitivity. Both types play a crucial role in various applications, including pharmaceuticals, environmental testing, and food safety analysis.</p></p>
