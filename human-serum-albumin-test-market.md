<p><p>The Human Serum Albumin Test market is segmented into two main types: recombinant and plasma-derived albumin. Recombinant human serum albumin is produced through biotechnological methods, ensuring a high level of purity and consistency, making it suitable for various medical applications. In contrast, plasma-derived albumin is extracted from human blood plasma, providing a natural source but potentially introducing variability and safety concerns. Both types are essential in therapeutic settings, including volume replacement and drug formulation.</p></p>
