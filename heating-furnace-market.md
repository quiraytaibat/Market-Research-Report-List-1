<p><p>The heating furnace market is primarily divided into two types: combustion and electric. Combustion-type furnaces rely on fuel sources like natural gas, oil, or propane to generate heat, making them efficient for larger spaces or applications requiring high heat outputs. In contrast, electric furnaces utilize electric resistance to produce heat, offering advantages such as easier installation, lower maintenance, and cleaner operation. Both types cater to residential and commercial needs, with selection often based on energy costs, efficiency, and environmental considerations.</p></p>
