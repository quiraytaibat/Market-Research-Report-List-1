<p><p>The permanent marker market is primarily divided into two types: liquid and solid permanent markers. Liquid permanent markers utilize ink stored in a reservoir, offering vibrant colors and versatility for various surfaces. They are commonly used in art, labeling, and crafting. Solid permanent markers, on the other hand, feature a wax or oil-based core that allows for a long-lasting mark with less risk of smudging. They are ideal for outdoor use, providing durability against water and fading. Both types cater to diverse user needs.</p></p>
