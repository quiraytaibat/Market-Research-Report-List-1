<p><p>The firestop bandages market is primarily categorized into two types: intumescent material and insulation material. Intumescent materials expand when exposed to heat, forming a protective char that seals gaps and prevents fire spread. These are ideal for applications where rapid response to heat is critical. On the other hand, insulation materials provide thermal resistance and minimize heat transfer. They are used to enhance fire safety in various constructions by maintaining structural integrity during high-temperature incidents.</p></p>
