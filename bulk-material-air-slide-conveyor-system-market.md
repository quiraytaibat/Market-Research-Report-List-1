<p><p>The Bulk Material Air Slide Conveyor System market is categorized into three main types: </p><p>1. **Dilute Phase Conveyor**: This system transports materials with a low density and pressure, using high air velocity to keep particles suspended. </p><p>2. **Dense Phase Conveyor**: Utilizes lower air velocities and higher pressure to move materials in a dense stream, minimizing dust generation and wear. </p><p>3. **Semi-Dense Phase Conveyor**: Balances pressure and air velocity to provide a versatile solution for a variety of bulk materials, offering benefits of both dilute and dense phases.</p></p>
