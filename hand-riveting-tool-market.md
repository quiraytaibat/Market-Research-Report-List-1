<p><p>The hand riveting tool market encompasses two primary types: lever riveters and manually activated rivet guns. Lever riveters utilize a mechanical lever to apply force, making them ideal for larger rivets and thicker materials, providing significant mechanical advantage and efficiency. Conversely, manually activated rivet guns operate through a simple trigger mechanism, offering portability and ease of use for smaller jobs. Both tools are essential in various industries for assembling components with rivets, ensuring strong and durable joints in construction and manufacturing applications.</p></p>
