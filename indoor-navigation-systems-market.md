<p><p>Indoor Navigation Systems utilize various technologies to provide precise location tracking and navigation within buildings. UWB (Ultra-Wideband) offers high accuracy and minimal interference, making it suitable for real-time tracking. Wi-Fi uses existing wireless networks for location services, balancing coverage and accuracy. Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) is cost-effective for proximity-based applications. RFID (Radio Frequency Identification) facilitates inventory tracking and asset management. Other technologies may include visual markers and geomagnetic approaches, expanding options for diverse navigation needs in indoor environments.</p></p>
