<p><p>Social media monitoring platforms are categorized mainly into two market types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based solutions operate on remote servers, allowing users to access monitoring tools via the internet. They offer scalability, automatic updates, and lower upfront costs, making them popular among businesses of all sizes. On-premises platforms, on the other hand, are installed locally on a company's servers, providing greater control over data security and customization. However, they typically require higher initial investment and ongoing maintenance.</p></p>
