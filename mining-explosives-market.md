<p><p>The mining explosives market features several key types crucial for efficient rock fragmentation. Ammonium Nitrate Explosives (powder) are known for their high energy output and application flexibility. ANFO (Ammonium Nitrate Fuel Oil) combines ammonium nitrate and fuel oil, providing cost-effective blasting solutions favored in many mining operations. Emulsion explosives, characterized by their water resistance and safety, offer superior performance in wet conditions, enhancing blasting effectiveness and minimizing environmental impact. Each type serves specific operational needs in the mining industry.</p></p>
