<p><p>The Single Orifice Air Valves market is primarily categorized into flanged and threaded types. Flanged air valves feature a flat rim that provides a secure connection to piping systems, ensuring stability and ease of maintenance, ideal for larger installations. In contrast, threaded air valves use screws or bolts for attachment, offering flexibility and a compact design suitable for smaller systems. Both types are essential for managing air release and preventing water hammer in various industrial applications, enhancing system efficiency.</p></p>
