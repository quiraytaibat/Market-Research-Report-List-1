<p><p>The foam hair dye market is segmented into three main types: Temporary, Semi- & Demi-Permanent, and Permanent hair dyes. Semi- & Demi-Permanent dyes provide longer-lasting color, typically fading after several washes, suitable for enhancing natural shades.
