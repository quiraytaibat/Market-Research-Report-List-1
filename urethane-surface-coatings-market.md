<p><strong>The Urethane Surface Coatings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Powder</li><li>Solvent-Borne</li><li>Water-Borne</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The urethane surface coatings market is categorized into three main types: powder, solvent-borne, and water-borne coatings. Powder coatings are free from solvents and provide a durable finish through an electrostatic application process, making them eco-friendly. Solvent-borne coatings utilize organic solvents, offering excellent adhesion and flexibility but may emit volatile organic compounds (VOCs). Water-borne coatings use water as a solvent, reducing environmental impact while providing good performance and easier cleanup. Each type serves specific applications in various industries.</p></p>
