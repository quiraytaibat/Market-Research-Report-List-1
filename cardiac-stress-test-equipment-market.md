<p><p>The cardiac stress test equipment market is primarily categorized into two types: treadmill-based and exercise bike-based systems. Treadmill stress tests simulate real-world walking or running scenarios, providing a comprehensive analysis of cardiovascular response to exercise. Conversely, exercise bike tests allow for controlled cycling, making them suitable for patients who may struggle with traditional treadmill testing. Both modalities are essential for diagnosing heart-related issues, offering different advantages based on patient needs and physical capabilities.</p></p>
