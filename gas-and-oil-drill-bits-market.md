<p><p>The Gas and Oil Drill Bits market comprises two primary types: Fixed Cutter and Roller Cone. Fixed Cutter bits, made from materials like diamond or carbide, utilize sharp edges to cut through rock formations and provide precision drilling. Conversely, Roller Cone bits feature rotating cones with steel teeth that crush rock as they turn, making them suitable for various geological conditions. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing drilling efficiency and adapting to different subsurface challenges in oil and gas exploration.</p></p>
