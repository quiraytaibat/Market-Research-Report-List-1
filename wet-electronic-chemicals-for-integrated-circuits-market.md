General wet electronic chemicals include solvents, acids, and bases used in various cleaning and etching processes, ensuring a contaminant-free substrate. Functional wet electronic chemicals, on the other hand, serve specific roles, such as dopants or photoresists, crucial for defining circuit patterns or modifying electrical properties. Both types are vital for enhancing the efficiency and performance of semiconductor manufacturing processes.</p></p>
