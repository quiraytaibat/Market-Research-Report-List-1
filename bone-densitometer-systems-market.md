<p><strong>The Bone Densitometer Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Axial Bone Densitometry</li><li>Peripheral Bone Densitometry</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Bone Densitometer Systems market is primarily divided into two types: Axial Bone Densitometry and Peripheral Bone Densitometry. Axial Bone Densitometry, which includes tools like DXA (Dual-Energy X-ray Absorptiometry), measures bone density in central regions such as the spine and hip, providing critical insights for osteoporosis diagnosis. Peripheral Bone Densitometry, on the other hand, evaluates bone density in peripheral sites like the wrist or heel, offering a quicker, portable option for initial assessments and screenings.</p></p>
