The in-line market involves devices installed along pipelines to stop flames from traveling through gas or vapor streams during operational processes. In contrast, the end-of-line market includes arrestors placed at the terminal ends of pipelines, ensuring any reverse flame travel is extinguished before it can enter equipment or facilities. Both types are crucial for managing fire hazards in various industries.</p></p>
