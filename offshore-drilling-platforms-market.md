<p><strong>The Offshore Drilling Platforms Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Jack-ups</li><li>Semi-submersibles</li><li>Drillships</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Offshore drilling platforms are classified into three main types: Jack-ups, Semi-submersibles, and Drillships. Jack-ups consist of a mobile platform supported by legs that can be jacked up to work in shallow waters. Semi-submersibles are floating platforms anchored to the seabed, designed for deeper waters, offering stability in rough seas. Drillships are highly mobile vessels equipped with drilling apparatus, capable of operating in ultra-deep water and are versatile for various drilling activities, adapting to changing conditions efficiently.</p></p>
