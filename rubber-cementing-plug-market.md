<p><strong>The Rubber Cementing Plug Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bottom Cementing Plug</li><li>Top Cementing Plug</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rubber cementing plug market is divided into two primary types: bottom cementing plugs and top cementing plugs. Bottom cementing plugs are utilized to prevent migration of cement in the wellbore during the placement of cement at the bottom of the casing, ensuring a secure barrier. Top cementing plugs, on the other hand, are employed to seal off the top portion of the cement column, preventing contamination and ensuring efficient cement setting. Both types are crucial in ensuring the integrity of well structures.</p></p>
