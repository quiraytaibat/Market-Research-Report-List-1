<p><strong>The Surgical Drainage Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active Drainage Devices</li><li>Passive Drainage Devices</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Surgical drainage devices are categorized into active and passive drainage devices. Active drainage devices utilize a vacuum or suction mechanism to facilitate the removal of fluids from a surgical site, promoting faster healing and minimizing complications. In contrast, passive drainage devices rely on gravity and natural pressure differences to allow fluids to accumulate in a collection container. Each type serves specific clinical needs, with active devices often preferred for more complex or high-volume drainage requirements, while passive devices are simpler and less expensive.</p></p>
