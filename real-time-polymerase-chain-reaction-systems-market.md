<p><p>The Real-Time Polymerase Chain Reaction (RT-PCR) Systems market comprises two main types: Non-Specific Detection and Specific Detection. Non-Specific Detection methods amplify all target nucleic acids present in a sample without distinguishing between different sequences, making them useful for broad applications. In contrast, Specific Detection focuses on particular DNA or RNA sequences, allowing for precise identification and quantification of targeted pathogens or genetic material. This specificity enhances analytical sensitivity and reliability in research and clinical diagnostics applications.</p></p>
