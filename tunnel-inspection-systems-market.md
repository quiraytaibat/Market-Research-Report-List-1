<p><p>The Tunnel Inspection Systems market encompasses technologies used to assess the structural integrity and safety of various tunnel types, including cut and cover, bored, and immersed tunnels. Cut and cover tunnels, typically constructed by excavating a trench, require regular inspections to monitor surface deformation and structural stability. Bored tunnels, created using tunnel boring machines, necessitate internal inspections for alignment and defects. Immersed tunnels, constructed by sinking pre-fabricated sections into water bodies, require thorough checks for water ingress and structural integrity post-installation.</p></p>
