<p><p>Adoptive Cellular Immunotherapy involves various types tailored to enhance immune responses against cancer. Tumor-Infiltrating Lymphocytes (TIL) use immune cells from tumors. Lymphokine-Activated Killer cells (LAK) are modified to target tumors. Chimeric Antigen Receptor T cells (CAR-T) are engineered for specific antigen recognition. T Cell Receptor T cells (TCR-T) target specific cancer antigens. Cytokine-Induced Killer cells (CIK) boost immune activity. Natural Killer cells (NK) attack tumors directly, while Dendritic cells (DC) stimulate immune responses. Other types include expanded and modified cell therapies.</p></p>
