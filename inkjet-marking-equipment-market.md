<p><p>Inkjet marking equipment encompasses various technologies used for coding and marking products. Continuous Inkjet (CIJ) systems excel in high-speed applications, continuously circulating ink to create images on various surfaces. Drop-On-Demand (DOD) technology, in contrast, ejects ink droplets only when needed, offering precision for intricate designs. CIJ is preferred for high-volume production, while DOD is ideal for variable data printing and smaller runs. Both types enhance branding and traceability in industries like packaging, pharmaceuticals, and automotive.</p></p>
