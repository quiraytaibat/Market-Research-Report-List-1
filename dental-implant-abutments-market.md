<p><strong>The Dental Implant Abutments Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Straight Abutments</li><li>Angled Abutments</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dental implant abutments market comprises primarily two types: straight abutments and angled abutments. Straight abutments are designed for cases where the implant is aligned perpendicular to the restorative tooth, providing a direct connection for crowns and prosthetics. Angled abutments, on the other hand, are used when implants are positioned at an angle, allowing for better alignment of the restoration with adjacent teeth. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring optimal aesthetic and functional outcomes in dental implants.</p></p>
