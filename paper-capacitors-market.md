<p><strong>The Paper Capacitors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Paper Sheet Capacitors</li><li>Metalized Paper Capacitors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The paper capacitors market comprises primarily two types: paper sheet capacitors and metalized paper capacitors. Paper sheet capacitors utilize thin sheets of paper as dielectric material and are known for higher voltage applications, offering reliability and stability. In contrast, metalized paper capacitors feature a thin metal layer deposited on the paper, enhancing capacitance and allowing for lower profiles while reducing size. Both types are valued for their unique electrical properties, cost-effectiveness, and versatility across various electronic applications.</p></p>
